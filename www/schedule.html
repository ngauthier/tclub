Schedule-Related Info

Rec

This means the Rec Center MultiPurpose Room. Go in the main Rec Center Entrance, and go up the stairs by the elevator (if you take the elevator you may be mocked). We are in the first room with the big glass double doors.

ROTC

This is the ROTC Room, which is behind the Rec Center. If you are approaching the Rec Center from the freshman quad, continue past the Rec Center keeping it on your left. Go through the track so that the bleachers are on your left. Go down the grassy hill right after the bleachers and the ROTC room is in through the unmarked white doors.

Team

This is a subset of TKD Club. Tryouts will be announced at practice.

Beginners

Beginners are white belts and new people without belts.

Intermediate

Intermediates are yellow and green belts.

Advanced

Advanced belts are blue and above.
